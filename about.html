About Our Business

Ethiopian Coffee Hub is a coffee business dedicated to promoting the rich taste and quality of authentic Ethiopian coffee. Ethiopia is known worldwide for producing some of the finest Arabica coffee beans, and our mission is to connect coffee lovers, cafés, and businesses with premium coffee products directly sourced from local farmers.

Our company works closely with coffee growers across Ethiopia to ensure that our coffee beans are ethically sourced, carefully processed, and roasted to perfection. By supporting local farmers and maintaining high-quality standards, we aim to strengthen Ethiopia’s reputation as a leading coffee producer in Africa.


Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver high-quality coffee products while supporting sustainable agriculture and empowering local coffee farmers. We strive to provide customers with exceptional coffee experiences and promote Ethiopia’s coffee industry both locally and internationally.


Our Vision

Our vision is to become one of the most trusted coffee brands in Ethiopia and a recognized exporter of premium Ethiopian coffee globally. We aim to build strong relationships with customers, businesses, and communities through quality, innovation, and sustainability.


Our Services

  • Coffee Bean Supply: We provide high-quality roasted and packaged Ethiopian coffee beans for households, cafés, and businesses.
  • Coffee Roasting: Our professional roasting process ensures rich aroma, balanced flavor, and premium quality coffee.
  • Wholesale Distribution: We supply coffee products to restaurants, cafés, supermarkets, and local retailers.
  • Coffee Tasting Experiences: Visitors and coffee enthusiasts can explore different Ethiopian coffee flavors through guided tasting sessions.
  • Business Partnerships: We collaborate with local businesses and hospitality companies to promote Ethiopian coffee products.
